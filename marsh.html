Marshmellow Challenge

In this lesson we were told to create the tallest tower we could with only a couple peices of noodles, a string, and some tape. The test was to see if a marshmellow could stand on top. Our group pretty much failed. Our noodles started to break and we did not put enough support at the base. This was a great learning experience in how each of our projects in this couse will take preplanning and may end up with a lot of failures.
