MakerBot Replicator 5th Gen printer Rules and Limitations


I used the limitation test printed out by Mr. Gerber. I found that:

  • maximum fineness of a peak is 1.55 mm
  • minimum thickness of a rounded off standing rectangle is 1.2 mm
  • minimum width of a cut is .45 mm
  • the file must be in .stl as .makerbot
  • the object must be flat on the platform
  • the hollow or opening part must be on the top when it prints
