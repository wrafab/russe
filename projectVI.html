Stamp

The project we to create a rubber stamp. I used Corel Draw to design it and cut it with the laser cutter.

I got a clip art turtle and pasted it into the dimensions of the stamp I wanted. Once it was printed on the

rubber, I cut it to fit the size of the wooden stamp piece. I then glued it on and created a very functional

stamp! This was a success!
