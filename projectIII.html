Phone Case


I used the Roland VersaUV LEF 20 to print out the design I made using Photoshop and Illustrator.



I cut out an image of Kyrie Irving and made the background transparent. I then cut out the skyline of Cleveland and pasted it below him. I wrote "Cleveland" above him and edited it to make it curved and faded.

The image came out too small and off center. I did not set the dimensions correctly when I created the design.
